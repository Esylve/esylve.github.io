Volv: The Revolving Refrigerator

Aug 2019 - Dec 2019

Completed for ISEN 489: 3D Design

Because most refrigerators are not designed at a height accessible to those in wheelchairs, our team designed Volv, which is a refrigerator with shelves designed to rotate down to the user. It was designed in Solidworks to meet and exceed ADA accessibility guidelines.

Skills Excercised:

  • Accessibility
  • Solidworks
  • Requirement Specifications
  • Product Lifecycle Management
  • ADA Requirements
